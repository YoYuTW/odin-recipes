Fried Rice

Description

An astonishing local dishes in Taiwan. Combine the ingridients you like, and make sure rice is covered by everything's flavor in the wok. Just that easy, but really delicious.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Eggs
  • Soysauce
  • Spices
  • Protein you enjoyed

Steps

  1. Seperate egg white and yok, mix the rice with yok.
  2. Heat up oil.
  3. Fry egg white until solid, put it on the plate, waiting for using.
  4. Put spices in wok, make sure nothing is burnt.
  5. If the protein is not seafood, put it in the wok. Also don't burn it up.
  6. When the smell comes out, put rice in the wok.
  7. Keep stiring rice and ingridients until turning to gold, add some soysauce and egg white.
  8. Done!
